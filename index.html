Our Coffee Journey

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small passion project between friends who shared a love for exceptional coffee. Today, we've grown into a community hub where coffee enthusiasts gather to savor carefully crafted beverages.

We source our beans directly from ethical farms across mombasa to Nairobi,roasting them in-house to bring out their unique characteristics. Every cup is brewed with precision and care by our skilled baristas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cafe, coffee, and services.

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website, by calling us directly at (+254) 700350007, or by visiting our cafe in person.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a variety of gluten-free pastries and snacks available daily. Our menu clearly marks gluten-free items, and our staff is trained to answer any dietary questions you may have.

Where do you source your coffee beans?

We partner directly with small scale farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Brazil who practice sustainable farming.
